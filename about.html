About Marathon

Nitte Marathon - Run for Fitness, Run for Life

Event Overview

Nitte Marathon 2026 is a large-scale fitness event designed to promote health, unity, and sportsmanship. It brings together students, professionals, and fitness enthusiasts across Bengaluru.

🏃 Mission

To promote fitness culture and encourage people to adopt a healthy lifestyle through running events.

🎯 Vision

To become one of the most inspiring and largest college marathon events in Karnataka.

📍 Event Info

📌 Location: Bengaluru
📅 Year: 2026
🏁 Categories: 5K & 10K Run
👥 Open for all age groups

🏁 Previous Marathon Experience

Nitte Marathon has successfully organized multiple running events over the years, attracting students, professional runners, fitness enthusiasts, and families from different parts of Karnataka.

Every year, the event grows bigger with better arrangements, improved race routes, medical support, hydration stations, live music, photography, and energetic crowd participation.

📅 Marathon Journey

🏃 2022 Event

More than 1200 participants joined the marathon. The event focused on fitness awareness among students and youth.

🏅 2023 Event

Introduced professional timing systems, medals, refreshment zones, and separate age categories.

🔥 2024 Event

Over 3000 runners participated. Social media promotions and sponsor partnerships made the event successful.

✨ Event Highlights

🎵 Live Energy

DJ music, motivational announcements, and cheering volunteers create a highly energetic environment.

🚑 Safety Support

Ambulance services, medical teams, water stations, and route volunteers ensure runner safety.

📸 Media Coverage

Professional photographers and media teams capture memorable moments throughout the marathon route.
