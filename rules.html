📜 Rules & Regulations

General Rules

  • All participants must register before the event.
  • Participants must carry a valid ID proof.
  • Entry without registration is not allowed.
  • Follow instructions given by organizers and volunteers.

Race Rules

  • Participants must wear the official bib number.
  • Cutting the route will lead to disqualification.
  • Use only designated race paths.
  • Follow all safety guidelines during the race.

Age Categories

  • Under 18 (5K)
  • 18 - 30
  • 30 - 45
  • Above 45

Health & Safety

  • Participants should be medically fit.
  • Stay hydrated during the race.
  • Medical assistance will be available on the route.

Disqualification

  • Cheating or unfair practices will lead to disqualification.
  • Misbehavior with staff or participants is not allowed.
